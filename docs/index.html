Waypoints

A Personal BioGeography

Welcome to my map of memories! This is an evolving project that captures the experiences and influences that have shaped me.

Feel free to toggle layers, pan, zoom, and explore the map. If multiple markers overlap, zooming in or toggling layers can help. Videos can be unmuted for a richer experience. If you encounter any bugs, please let me know!

This map is deeply personal—a way to share my journey and, perhaps, bore you with my vacation photos! It’s a collection of memories that reflect the places and moments that have left an impact on me.

One thing I’ve noticed: shorter trips often result in more coherent photo narratives. The longer I stay in a place, the fewer photos I take, and the less structured the story becomes. Someday, I’ll add captions and organize the photos better.

The next phase of this project is to include travel routes—hikes, train rides, road trips, and more. Stay tuned!

Thank you to everyone who has shared these experiences with me. I hope you enjoy exploring this map as much as I’ve enjoyed creating it!
